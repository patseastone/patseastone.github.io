You’ve decided to start going to the gym. Or to start again. Either way, the hardest part isn’t the weights — it’s the not‑knowing.

The Complete Handbook is the gym guide that treats you like an adult. No mandatory exercises. No bro‑science. No rigid twelve‑week programme that assumes your body works exactly like everyone else’s. Instead, it gives you the principles that underpin every effective training programme — and the freedom to build something that actually fits your life.

  • The Quick Start Card — the absolute minimum you need to walk into a gym today and leave feeling like you did something right.
  • Complete beginner guidance — from understanding soreness and building confidence, to navigating the gym floor, warming up, and recovering well.
  • The principles behind the work — rep ranges, progressive overload, and how small changes in your grip or stance can shift the focus of an exercise, all explained clearly and calmly.
  • Over 70 illustrated exercises with a session builder that puts you in control — choose the movements you enjoy and build sessions around them.
  • “If not this, then that” alternatives for every exercise, because you should never feel stuck doing something you hate.
  • A gentle roadmap for your first six months, a troubleshooting guide for when things go wrong, and a simple training log to track your progress.

Whether you’re walking through the door for the first time, or returning after years away and wanting to lift on your own terms — this is where you start. No shouting. No rigid plans. Just the next right step.
