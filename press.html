Description

You've decided to start going to the gym. You just don't know what to do when you get there.

The Complete Handbook is the gym guide for people who've never lifted before — and who are tired of books that hand them a rigid twelve‑week programme and expect them to follow it exactly.

No mandatory exercises. No bro‑science. Over 70 illustrated exercises, a session builder you control, and the first gym guide that trusts you to make your own decisions.

Whether you want to build muscle, get stronger, or simply feel at home in the gym — this is where you start.

Author Bio

Pat Seastone is a fitness writer dedicated to making the weight room accessible. Disillusioned by bro‑science, aggressive fitness culture, and rigid 12‑week programmes, Pat writes warm, permission‑first books that trust beginners to make their own choices. The Complete Handbook is Pat's definitive guide to lifting on your own terms.
